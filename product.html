    PVC & FRP Doors

    PVC doors or plastic doors are mostly used as toilet and bathroom doors owing to the water-proof nature of plastic. These doors are completely termite and water resistant and are the most eco-friendly and pocket friendly option for projects and spaces where moisture is an issue and water/termite/fungus resistance is needed. PVC doors are made using injection moulding machines and are completely factory made.

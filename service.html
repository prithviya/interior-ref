    Some Special Services

    • Interior Coloring

      Colour is one of the most fundamental and influential aspects of interior design. Colour selection is one of the important works in interior design. Colour can also be used to change the apparent size, making small rooms appear as larger rooms or spacious rooms seem smaller. Colour can give the focus of the decorative scheme, or it can be used purely as a backdrop.

    • Interior Lighting

      Lighting is one of the important things in interior design. We use light and shade to make a room feel comfortable.It's all about the balance of light and shade and bringing new energy to an interior. A flexible light will always have a mix of decorative and architectural lighting. The lighting fixture has a variety of styles for various functions.

    • Texture Interior

      Texture in interior design makes an object’s physical feeling or visual appearance. Layering in interior design will make the design rich and good-looking.Textures help to keep a space from feeling flat or one dimensional. It's all about the balance of light and shade and bringing new energy to an interior. The texture will add visual weight to the interior.
